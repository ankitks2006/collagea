Mixed Integer Programming (MIP) Workshop

The inaugural International Mixed Integer Programming (MIP) Workshop took place from December 2–6, 2024, at site, India. It is a single-track workshop series highlighting the latest trends in mixed-integer programming and discrete optimization. Featuring invited talks, poster sessions for students and postdocs, and vibrant discussions, the workshop fostered collaboration and highlighted innovative applications in the field.
